Lawrence Juma

Founder & CEO

Lawrence is based in Western Kenya, with almost a decade of experience in Project Management and Research. Since 2020, he has been the technical advisor for the Toyota Mobility Foundation through the Kisii Smart Community Project, leading country-level research on the adoption and impact of Electric Vehicles in rural smallholder agriculture in Western Kenya. Previously, he consulted for Educate! between 2017 and 2019, leading program design, monitoring and evaluation processes through Design Thinking methodologies. Connect on LinkedIn

Juliah Omariba

Co-Founder and COO

Based in Kisii, Kenya, Juliah is a dynamic professional with proven expertise in optimizing business operations and implementing solutions that drive both social and economic transformation. Since March 2021, she has led the groundbreaking Kisii Smart Community project, leveraging off-grid renewable energy, clean mobility, and technology solutions to improve rural communities' quality of life.
